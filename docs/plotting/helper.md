Figures
===

Thin wrappers around matplotlib for consistent figure creation and export:
`fig_ax` applies the paper's default figsize, `legend_upper_center`/
`legend_upper_right` place legends above the axes, `mark_axis_break`/`row_title` help build axes broken along the x- or y-axis,
and `save_plot` writes a figure to `FIGURES_DIR` as both PNG and PDF.
