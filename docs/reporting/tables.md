Tables
===

Helpers for building [Great Tables](https://posit-dev.github.io/great-tables/)
tables: `get_col_name_map` recovers a lookup from polars pivot-generated
column names, `add_spanner` applies spanners from such a mapping, and
`fix_gt_table` adapts Great Tables' LaTeX output to this project's paper
layout.
